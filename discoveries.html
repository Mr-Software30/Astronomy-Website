Major Discoveries

1. Proxima b

First confirmed microbial life discovered beneath the icy crust.

2. Kepler-442b

Lush forests and bioluminescent organisms found in the twilight zone.

3. TRAPPIST-1e

Complex aquatic life and evidence of ancient civilizations.

4. Gliese 581g

Unique silicon-based lifeforms thriving in extreme environments.

5. Enceladus Ocean

Discovery of hydrothermal vents supporting extremophile bacteria.

6. Titan Lakes

Organic molecules forming the basis for prebiotic chemistry.

7. Europa Ridge

Subsurface ocean with evidence of simple multicellular organisms.

8. Mars Valles Marineris

Ancient riverbeds containing fossilized microbial mats.

9. LHS 1140 b

Atmosphere rich in oxygen and signs of photosynthetic life.

10. GJ 667 Cc

Discovery of crystalline lifeforms based on ammonia metabolism.

11. Wolf 1061c

Dense jungles with rapid-evolving insect-like species.

12. Ross 128 b

Complex cave ecosystems with bioluminescent fungi.

13. K2-18b

Water vapor clouds and floating microbial colonies.

14. HD 40307g

Discovery of magnetic field-driven auroras supporting unique energy cycles.

15. Tau Ceti e

Desert planet with resilient plant analogues and water reservoirs.

16. 55 Cancri e

Carbon-rich surface with diamond-based microbial life.

17. Barnard's Star b

Discovery of ancient ice structures with preserved DNA fragments.

18. GJ 273b

Subterranean biospheres with heat-loving archaea.

19. WASP-12b

Atmospheric chemistry indicating possible silicon-based photosynthesis.

20. Kepler-62f

Oceans teeming with plankton-like organisms and complex food webs.
